Overview

Artha ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our personal finance management application.

Our core principle is simple: Your financial data belongs to you. We've built Artha with a privacy-first, local-first architecture.

Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication
  • Financial Data: Transactions, categories, goals, budgets (stored locally on your device)
  • Preferences: App settings, currency, theme choices
  • Device Info: Basic device identifiers for crash reporting (optional)

‚úì Local-First: All your financial data is stored locally on your device by default. We do NOT have access to your transaction data unless you explicitly enable cloud backup.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256 Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted at rest
  • Biometric Authentication: Fingerprint and Face ID support
  • No Cloud by Default: Data stays on your device
  • End-to-End Encryption: If you enable cloud backup
  • SSL/TLS: All network communications are encrypted
  • No Tracking: We don't use analytics on your financial data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download your data in CSV or JSON format

  • Consent: Opt-in/out of optional features like backup

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app:

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Edit Profile ‚Üí Delete Account
  2. Confirm your decision in the dialog

When you delete your account:

  • All local data is immediately deleted from your device
  • Your authentication account is removed from our servers
  • Any cloud backup data is deleted within 30 days
  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone

If you need assistance with account deletion, contact us at support@artha.app

Cloud Backup (Optional)

  • You can delete cloud backups anytime
  • Data is retained for 90 days after account deletion
